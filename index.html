Common Questions

Answers Before
You Call

Is the treatment painful?
+
We use modern local anaesthesia for all procedures. Most patients feel little to no pain during treatment. Post-procedure discomfort, if any, is mild and managed with basic medication.
How much does treatment cost?
+
Costs vary by treatment type and complexity. We provide a detailed cost breakdown before any procedure begins — no hidden charges. EMI options are available to make it affordable.
How many visits will I need?
+
Simple procedures like scaling or fillings are done in one visit. Root canals typically take 1–2 visits. Implants need 2–3 visits over a few weeks. We'll give you a clear plan from the start.
Do you offer EMI?
+
Yes! We offer 0% EMI on select treatments. Ask our team about EMI eligibility during your consultation. We believe quality dental care should be accessible to everyone.
Is emergency treatment available?
+
Absolutely. We keep emergency slots available daily. If you're in acute pain, call us immediately and we'll get you seen the same day. Your pain is our priority.
Is the first consultation free?
+
Yes, the initial consultation with our dentist is complimentary. You'll receive a full examination, recommendations, and a cost estimate — all at no charge on your first visit.
